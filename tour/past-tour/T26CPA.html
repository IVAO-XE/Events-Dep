Welcome to “Life of a Pilot”

Introduction

Cathay Pacific (IATA: CX, ICAO: CPA, Callsign: CATHAY) is a major international airline based in Hong Kong, founded in 1946. Headquartered at Hong Kong Intl (VHHH), the carrier operates an extensive global network covering Asia, Europe, North America, and Oceania. As a founding member of the oneworld alliance, Cathay Pacific offers premium passenger and cargo services renowned for their quality, safety, and hospitality. The airline has been recognized globally for its world-class service and plays a key role in positioning Hong Kong as a leading international aviation hub.

In this tour, you will experience the life of an Cathay Pacific pilot, following real flight routes and flight numbers, based on data from October 2025.
