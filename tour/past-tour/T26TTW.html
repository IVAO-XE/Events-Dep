Welcome to “Life of a Pilot”

Introduction

Tigerair Taiwan (IATA: IT, ICAO: TTW, Callsign: SMART CAT) is a low-cost carrier based in Taoyuan, Taiwan. Established in 2013 as a joint venture between China Airlines and Tigerair Singapore, the airline operates from its main hub at Taiwan Taoyuan Intl (RCTP). Tigerair Taiwan focuses on short- to medium-haul routes across Asia, connecting Taiwan with popular destinations in Japan, South Korea, Thailand, the Philippines, and more. Tigerair Taiwan is currently the only Taiwanese low-cost airline.

In this tour, you will experience the life of a Tigerair Taiwan pilot, following real flight routes and flight numbers, based on data from October 2025.
