Individual Tour Rules for Life of an Airline Pilot (ITR.A)

  • Version: 1.0
  • Date of Issue: 2025/12/24
  • Effective Date: 2026/01/01
ℹ️ Even if you are familiar with these rules, please take a moment to review them carefully.
⚠ Failure to comply with the IVAO Rules and Regulations (R&R), the XE Individual Tour Rules (XE.ITR) for each specific tour, and the XE General Tour Rules (XE.GTR) will result in your report being rejected.

§ XE.ITR.A.1 - Callsign

Pilots shall use the designated callsign as specified in Leg List > Information.

§ XE.ITR.A.2 - Aircraft Type and Livery

Pilots shall fly this tour using any airliner jet. Other aircraft types, such as business jets or military aircraft, are prohibited. In addition, pilots are encouraged to use the same aircraft type and livery as that of the airline, but this is not mandatory.

ℹ️ Note
  • Pilots may refer to Airfleets.net to check an airline’s fleet information.

§ XE.ITR.A.3 - Flight Plan Remarks

Pilots shall include the following remark in RMK/ of their flight plan: T26AAR.

⚠ Caution
  • Use a space (“ ”) to separate multiple entries in RMK/. Example: RMK/WORLDTOUR TCAS SIMBRIEF.

§ XE.ITR.A.4 - Amendment and Interpretation Rights

The XE Events Department reserves the right to amend these Individual Tour Rules (ITR) and holds the final right of interpretation.

Should you have any inquiries, please contact the XE Events Department via email at xe-events@ivao.aero, or via the XE Discord server.
