Welcome to “Life of an Pilot”

Introduction

Asiana Airlines (IATA: OZ, ICAO: AAR, Callsign: ASIANA) is a major South Korean carrier founded in 1988. Headquartered in Seoul, the airline operates from its primary hubs at Incheon Intl (RKSI) and Gimpo Intl (RKSS). As a member of the Star Alliance, Asiana offers an extensive route network across Asia, Europe, North America, and Oceania, serving both passengers and cargo.

In this tour, you will experience the life of an Asiana Airlines pilot, following real flight routes and flight numbers, based on data from October 2025.
