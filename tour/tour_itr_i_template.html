§ XE.ITR.I.1 - Aircraft Type and Livery

Pilots shall fly this tour using any airliner jet. Other aircraft types, such as business jets or military aircraft, are prohibited.

§ XE.ITR.I.2 - Flight Plan Remarks

Pilots shall include the following remark in RMK/ of their flight plan: T26MOF.

⚠ Caution
  • Use a space (“ ”) to separate multiple entries in RMK/. Example: RMK/WORLDTOUR TCAS SIMBRIEF.

§ XE.ITR.I.3 - Amendment and Interpretation Rights

The XE Events Department reserves the right to amend these Individual Tour Rules (ITR) and holds the final right of interpretation.

Should you have any inquiries, please contact the XE Events Department via email at xe-events@ivao.aero, or via the XE Discord server.
